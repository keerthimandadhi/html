Social media applications

  • Facebook
    • a social networking platform that allows users to connect with friends and family
    • share posts and media
    • join groups and events
  • Twitter
    • a micro-blogging platform that allows users to post short messages called "tweets"
    • follow other users
    • engage in conversations using hashtags
  • Instagram
    • a photo and video sharing platform that allows users to post pictures and videos
    • follow other users
    • engage with content using likes and comments
  • TikTok
    • a short-form video sharing platform that allows users to create and share short videos set to music
    • engage with content using likes, comments, and shares
  • LinkedIn
    • a professional networking platform that allows users to create a profile
    • connect with other professionals
    • share content related to their industry
  • Reddit
    • a social news aggregation platform that allows users to submit and vote on content
    • join communities (called "subreddits")
    • engage in discussions
  • Snapchat
    • a multimedia messaging app that allows users to send pictures and videos that disappear after a set amount of time
    • engage with content using filters and lenses
